American Burger

Best burger ever homemade recipe

The ultimate american burger recipe you can do at home:

Ingredients

  • Fresh ground meat
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Letuce
  • Briosh bread
  • butter

Directions

  1. Toast the bread with butter(2 halfs)
  2. In a hot pan stear the burger till your taste
  3. Bottom bread place letuce, tomato ,onions, and finally the burger and cover with the top bread
  4. That's it. Bon apetit!
